Wordpress Instagram

== Description ==

A fantastic way to display instagram photos and videos on your website.
Wordpress Instagram plugin adds floating widget that shows photos and videos from #tag or from @user.
No extra knowledge needed, simple, useful and free

== Installation ==

Upload the Wordpress Instagram plugin to your blog. Activate it, DONE!

1, 2, 3: You're done!
